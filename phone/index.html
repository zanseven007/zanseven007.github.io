Welcome To Coda Showroom!

Coda Showroom is a Shanghai based fashion company specializing in strategic distribution placement, brand development, merchandising, and market data research – offering expertise in account management and marketing solution in branding, PR and retail coordination. We establish relationship with top stores and boutiques, expertise in retail logistics and production solution.

Services



Wholesale
Coda Showroom is focused on choosing leading designers with a strong brand identity and strength in design aesthetic. We assist brands in maximizing their wholesale in a brand right manner, accomplished by providing access to a significant and ever increasing group of retailers, and generating orders and brand recognition through sales professions in partnership with the designers. Coda Showroom invoke a higher standard of sales with designers and stores by their participation on private labels, trunk shows, clinics, window displays, Ad books, special projects and collaborations.

Marketing, Branding and Merchandising
Coda Showroom has its goal to grow lines in the most successful way to increase brand recognition while staying true to the integrity of the line. With the knowledge we provide in marketing, branding and merchandising, as wells as the plan to execute it, designers have grown to recognizable names in the fashion industry.

Brand Incubation
Our team has multiple levels of experience, knowledge and understanding in business, strategic distribution, placement, goals, selling, merchandising, follow-through and understanding the creative end of design. With this understanding and trust, we work with fashion brands to offer feedback and written business plans covering the entire range of business and creative needs. Through this we are able to wisely build businesses and grow them as brands.

Founded by designer Zhang Da in 2005, “Boundless” is aim to look for different possibilities in fashion design and to push the boundaries ever further. In the brand’s early years, Zhang Da applied the profound Chinese philosophy of “Done by Inaction” in his design. By using the human body as support points as well as its movements, he was able to create 3-dimentional variations from 2-dimentional cutting clothing.

The concept of the label is to explore the idea of “Time and Memory”. We aim to offer conceptual yet practical garments, to demonstrate the idea that fashion is a form of applied art which carries a profound significance.
One of the central ideas behind the label is that a garment not only depicts the passage of time but also forms an intrinsic bond between the wearer and their memories and experiences.

Museum of Friendship is a London based womenswear brand founded by Momo Wang, launched in February 2014.
Museum of Friendship's design collects and reflects unforgettable memories, emotions and experiences that are exclusively shared between 'girlfriends'. The designs narrate the stories of the girls. As they dress up and grow up together in Museum of Friendship, every single piece that they have worn will become the witness of a precious story about friendship.

Since its foundation in 2011 by designer Yunze Li ,The menswear of TACITURNLI always focus on expressing the fine and neat American casual style. The designer trys to apply textured material and unique colors to refresh the classical manufacture craft and create simple casual clothes with vintage spirit.
